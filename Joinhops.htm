Play for Wisconsin Hops

Are you interested in playing Club Ultimate? Wisconsin Hops is an Open Ultimate Frisbee Team primarily based out of Stevens Point Wisconsin, but members travel from all parts of the state. Founded in 2005, Hops ran for a 4 years before being disbanded. Hops then reemerged when Tom Hoeft wrangled together some of the best players across the state. Hops strive to create a positive ultimate culture in central Wisconsin for all elite players to participate in.
